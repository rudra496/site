Judging Criteria

How we evaluated the internal pilot prototypes

  • 30% Practicality

    Can this actually run on a standard student laptop or mobile device?

  • 30% Local Impact

    Does this solve a problem specific to Bangladesh or SUST?

  • 20% Execution

    How clean is the code, and how efficiently does it use APIs/local models?

  • 20% Presentation

    Can the team clearly explain the human value of the product?
