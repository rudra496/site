About Me

Hello! I'm Rudra.

I am a third-year Industrial and Production Engineering student at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST), Sylhet. I'm passionate about the intersection of technology, engineering, and creativity. My journey blends hands-on robotics builds, IoT projects, software development, and exploring how innovation shapes the world.

From optimizing supply chains to building intelligent robots and crafting seamless web applications, I am driven by a relentless curiosity to learn and build. My goal is to leverage my engineering background to solve meaningful problems and create impactful technology solutions.

When I'm not coding or studying, I'm often found tinkering with electronics, exploring new cybersecurity concepts, or contributing to my robotics club.
